Hi! I'm Jamie Carter, a full-stack engineer currently building thoughtful products at a remote-first startup. I love working across the stack, from the design of collaborative user experiences down to the nitty-gritty of API design and developer tooling.

This blog is my corner of the internet where I share what I'm learning, publish notes from talks, and document experiments that teach me something new. I try to publish every few weeks and focus on approachable writing that other curious builders can enjoy.

What I'm excited about

  • Developer experience and tools that empower small teams
  • Design systems and thoughtful interfaces
  • Open source communities and sustainable contribution models
  • Running, analog photography, and third-wave coffee
